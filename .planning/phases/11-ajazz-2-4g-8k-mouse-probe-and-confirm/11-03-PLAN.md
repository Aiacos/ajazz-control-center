static scaffolds with no backend binding). Every byte-level encode routes through the
`kAj24g8kEnvelope` switch-point from 11-01.

Output: poll-rate to 8000 Hz with an honest SOF-cap UI warning + descriptor-driven per-zone RGB,
MockTransport-asserted at the documented bytes — all capture-pending until the Phase 9.x envelope
confirmation.

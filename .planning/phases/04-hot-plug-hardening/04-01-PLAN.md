Output: `DevicePtr` is now a `shared_ptr` alias; all 7 factories return `shared_ptr`; `DeviceRegistry::open()` is a weak_ptr-cached flyweight with passive (weak_ptr-expiry) eviction and zombie-safe sentinel returns from `IDevice` methods.
